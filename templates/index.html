VForU – We are for You.

Are you suffering from Depression? Anxiety? Suicidal thoughts? Don’t worry. We got you.

What's VForU

Everyone goes through something that the world doesn’t see.V for U is available 24*7 to hear your thoughts,

pain, and sorrows. Problems with family, relationships, and society have been arising so much in today’s world.

We understand that not everyone has a companion/partner to vent out completely.

V for U is a platform for you to connect with listeners who are ready to listen to you and be there for you in need.

Our team aims to connect you with people who have been striving to help individuals in their hard times.

All you need to do is join V for U as a member and you will be given the right to choose of speaking with various listeners listed.

Our listeners are experts in listening and handling individuals who need company anytime. You get to choose from categories of

Family, Relationship, and LGBTQ issues.

The steps are simple,

1.Create an account with us by going to the Sign Up page.

2.Login with your new credentials.

3.Find all the available listeners from the three categories stated above.

4.Choose the listener you want.

5.Start chatting with your listener until you feel you are okay.
